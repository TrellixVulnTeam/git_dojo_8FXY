Ninja: Path to Riches

How to play:

Goal: Reach 50 gold without losing more than 50g!


Pick and choose 4 different paths:

  • Duelist(Duel) - Gain up to 5 gold or lose up to 5 gold.
  • Adventurer(Quest) - Gain up to 20 gold or lose up to 20 gold.
  • Gambler(Bakuto) - small bet(costs 5g): can win up to 20g, medium bet(costs 10g): win up to 30g, large bet(costs 20g): win up to 50g.
  • Raider(Raid) - chance to gain up to 50 gold or lose up to 50 gold.
